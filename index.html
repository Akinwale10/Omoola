Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers!

Do you offer delivery services?

+

Yes! We offer fast and reliable delivery services within our service areas. Delivery fees vary based on location. Orders above a certain amount qualify for free delivery. Contact us for specific details about your area.

What are your opening hours?

+

We are open Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 9:00 PM, and Sunday from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Our pharmacy operates during the same hours with licensed pharmacists always available.

How do I submit a prescription?

+

You can submit your prescription in three ways: 1) Visit our store in person, 2) Upload it through our website (coming soon), or 3) Send it via WhatsApp to our pharmacy number. Our pharmacists will review and prepare your medication promptly.

What is your return/refund policy?

+

For groceries: Items can be returned within 24 hours if unopened and in original condition. For medications: Due to health regulations, medications cannot be returned once dispensed. However, if there's an error from our end, we will resolve it immediately. Please contact us for specific concerns.

How does the food ordering process work?

+

Simply browse our food menu, select your items, and place your order via phone, WhatsApp, or in-store. You can choose pickup or delivery. Preparation time varies from 20-45 minutes depending on the meal. We accept cash and mobile payments.

Do you accept health insurance for pharmacy purchases?

+

Yes, we accept major health insurance plans. Please bring your insurance card and valid ID when purchasing medications. Our pharmacy staff will assist with processing your insurance claims.

Can I order in bulk for events or organizations?

+

Absolutely! We offer special pricing for bulk orders and corporate clients. Contact our sales team to discuss your requirements, and we'll create a customized package for your event or organization.
